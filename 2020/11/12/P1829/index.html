P1829

发表于 2020-11-12 | 分类于 题解

Meaning

求出 \[ \sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m lcm(i,j) \]

Sol

\[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m \frac{ij}{\gcd( i,j)}&= \sum_{d=1}^n \frac{1}{d}\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{d}\rfloor }\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{m}{d}\rfloor } \frac{ij}{[\gcd(\frac{i}{d},\frac{j}{d})=1]}\\ &= \sum_{d=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{d}\rfloor}\frac{1}{d}\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{d}\rfloor }\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{m}{d}\rfloor } \sum _{k|\gcd(\frac{i}{d},\frac{j}{d})} \frac{1}{\mu(d)}ij\\ &= \text{???} \end{aligned} \]

哦, 推不下去了. cnmsb!

想一想, 为什么?

那咋办? 老老实实的枚举\(d\). \[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m \frac{ij}{\gcd( i,j)}&=\sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m\sum_{d=1}^n\frac{ij}{d}[\gcd (i,j)=d]\\ &=\sum_{i=1}^{\frac{n}{d}} \sum_{j=1}^{\frac{m}{d}}\sum_{d=1}^{n}\frac{ij}{d}[\gcd ({\frac{i}{d}},{\frac{j}{d}})=1]d^2 \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{\frac{n}{d}} \sum_{j=1}^{\frac{m}{d}}\sum_{d=1}^{n}ijd \sum_{k|\gcd({\frac{i}{d}},{\frac{j}{d}})} \mu(k)\\ &= \sum_{d=1}^{n} \sum_{k=1}^{\frac{n}{d}} \mu(k)\cdot k^2\sum_{i=1}^{\frac{n}{dk}}i\sum_{i=1}^{\frac{m}{dk}}j \end{aligned} \] 令\(T=dk,f(x)=\sum_{i=1}^{x}i\), 我们有: \[ \begin{align} \sum_{d = 1}^{n} \sum_{k = 1}^{\frac{n}{d}} \mu(k)\cdot k^2\sum_{i = 1}^{\frac{n}{dk}}i\sum_{i = 1}^{\frac{m}{dk}}j & =\sum_{d=1}^n \sum_{k=1}^{\frac{n}{d}}\mu(k)\cdot k^2 f(\frac{n}{T})f(\frac{m}{T})\\ &= \sum_{d=1}^n \sum_{k=1}^{\frac{n}{d}}\mu(k)\cdot k^2 \sum_{d|T} f(\frac{n}{T})f(\frac{m}{T})\\ &=\sum_{T=1}^{n}f(\lfloor\frac{n}{T}\rfloor)*f(\lfloor\frac{m}{T}\rfloor)\sum_{d|T}d*\mu(d)*T \end{align} \] 今天又看到了一篇欧拉反演的东西.但是遗憾的是, 如果在分母上出现这种sbgcd, 就不能用这东西.

搞死了,草. $$ \[\begin{aligned} & \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{m} \operatorname{lcm}(i, j) \\ =& \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{m} \frac{i j}{\operatorname{gcd}(i, j)} \\ =& \sum_{k=1}^{\min \{n, m\}} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{m} \frac{i j}{k}[\operatorname{gcd}(i, j)=k]\\ &=\sum_{k=1}^{\min \{n, m\}} \sum_{i=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\right\rfloor} \sum_{j=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k}\right\rfloor} \frac{(i k)(j k)}{k}[\operatorname{gcd}(i, j)=1]\\ &=\sum_{k=1}^{\min \{n, m\}} \sum_{i=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\right\rfloor} \sum_{j=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k}\right\rfloor} i j k \sum_{d \operatorname{gcd}(i, j)} \mu(d)\\ &=\sum_{k=1}^{\min \{n, m\}} \sum_{d=1}^{\min \left\{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\right\rfloor,\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k}\right\rfloor\right\}} \mu(d) \sum_{i=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\right\rfloor} \sum_{j=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k}\right\rfloor} i j k[d \mid i][d \mid j]\\ &=\sum_{k=1}^{\min \{n, m\}} \sum_{d=1}^{\min \left\{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\right\rfloor,\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k}\right\rfloor\right\}} \mu(d) \sum_{i=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k d}\right\rfloor} \sum_{j=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k a}\right\rfloor} i j k d^{2}\\ &=\sum_{k=1}^{\min \{n, m\}} k \sum_{d=1}^{\min \left\{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k}\right\rfloor,\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k}\right\rfloor\right\}} d^{2} \mu(d)\left(\sum_{i=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{n}{k d}\right\rfloor} i\right)\left(\sum_{j=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{m}{k a}\lfloor\right\rfloor} j\right)\\ &=\sum_{T=1}^{\min \{n, m\}}\left(\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor T\rfloor} i\right)\left(\sum_{j=1}^{\left.\frac{n}{T}\right\rfloor} j\right) \sum_{d \mid T} d^{2} \mu(d) \frac{T}{d}\\ &=\sum_{T=1}^{\min \{n, m\}} T\left(\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor T\rfloor} i\right)\left(\sum_{j=1}^{\left\lfloor\frac{m}{T}\right\rfloor} j\right) \sum_{d T} d \mu(d) \end{aligned}\]

$$ 傻逼题目,推这么长的式子.
